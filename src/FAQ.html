At the turn of the month we will become one big indeat! Pizza-online.fi will turn completely pink and we will all move to one app/webpage. Below are some of the most frequently asked questions about the rebranding.

Why are the two brands changing into one brand?

We always prioritize your experience with us as a customer. With one brand we can offer more restaurants, more cuisines,better service and an all-in-all better product for you! We hope you feel the same.

What do I as a customer need to do?

Pizza-online.fi customers: You donâ€™t need to do anything other than update your app.Pizza-online.fi will simply change into indeat and you can continue using the service with your Pizza-online.fi username and password. If you go to the webpage pizza-online.fi it will redirect you to the webpage indeat.fi.

indeat customers: Please download the new app with the name indeat Finland and login with your indeat account. If you already had a Pizza-online.fi account as well, that will be your indeat account in the future. The old indeat app should have a link which takes you to the app store where you can download the new app.

As a indeat customer, why do I need to download a new app?

Because of technical reasons we will rebrand the Pizza-online.fi app to indeat, thus we ask you to download the new app indeat Finland. As mentioned however, if you already have the Pizza-online.fi app you don't need to do anything - this app will be transformed into indeat Finland.

When will I get more info about the rebranding?

We will continuously inform you about all the upcoming changes through email, push notifications and messages in the app for each respective brand. Exact dates and timelines will also be shared continuously. If there is still something you are wondering, do not hesitate to contact our customer service!

Will my favorite restaurants disappear?

No, all your favorite restaurants will still be on the new platform. You will now also have access to way more alternatives!

Will my vouchers continue to work?

All vouchers that you received as a Pizza-online.fi customer will continue to work after the rebranding. Vouchers received for indeat customers will not work after the rebranding. Check your voucher to find out the specific end date.

What will happen to my bonuses?

No worries! Your bonuses will remain as they are. Nothing will change with your Pizza-online.fi user account or user information.

What if I am a corporate customer?

If you are using our service as a corporate customer, you might have to relogin but urls will remain the same for ordering (corporate.indeat.fi) and admin (corpadmin.indeat.fi/company). Your user history will unfortunately not be transferred.

Will my refer-a-friend credits get transferred to indeat Finland app?

Unfortunately not. We recommend you to use them before the 28th of January 2021.
